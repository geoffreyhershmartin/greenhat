Project GreenHat

Project GreenHat empowers people to decide what advertisements they see and choose where the revenue from those advertisements goes.

Help Your Cause Grow

Most causes and charities benefit much more from steady streams of donations. GreenHat provides charities with steady streams of income, allowing them to reliably grow.

  1. Download GreenHat
  2. Choose the cause or causes you want to help grow
  3. Build your cause as you browse the web

Shape Your Experience

Apart from donating to your cause of choice, GreenHat allows you to shape your browsing experience by choosing which advertisements you would like to see.

GreenHat does not store any of your browsing data, which insures a safe and risk-free experience


Building a Community of Giving

Because of how the online advertising industry works, the more users GreenHat has, the more advertising revenue is made by each click. By downloading GreenHat, you are not only donating to your cause, but also helping the global community.

How it Works

Curated Advertisements

Our team partners with the least distracting and most aesthetically pleasing advertising companies to provide content to replace the ads on your sceen.

Every View Counts

Every time you load a page and see our partners' content, they make a transfer to GreenHat

Make a Difference

We let you choose where the money goes. Just choose a cause you care about.

Help Causes Further Their Impact

Charities can vastly further their impact when they build steady sources of donations. Through GreenHat, you can help the Cause you care about grow.
